Hello World!

Posted by Shubham Chatterjee on July 29, 2019

Hello and welcome to my blog! First of all, a BIG Thank You to you for taking time to visit my blog. This is the first time I am blogging and I am really excited!

The idea of writing a blog about various topics in Machine Learning, Natural Language Proceessing and Data Science in general had been on my mind for quite some time now but I never got around to doing it. But you might ask, why bother to write a blog about data science and machine learning when there are plenty of really good stuff out there in the form of blogs, books and slides about the same topics. Well, I agree. BUT, it is REALLY difficult for me to keep track of all the vast amount of material out there. And not all of them explain all the stuff well. This is the place where I compile stuff from various sources and keep them handy for my own knowledge. Many times, I understand a concept really well and I need to have a place where I can write down what I understood and refer to it later when I forget (which by the way happens vey often!). So this blog is really my notebook to keep track of papers and the various concepts that I find difficult.

This is also the place where you will (hopefully) find material that is going to help you in that machine learning class that you are taking. I always feel that most of us tend to have the same doubts regarding the same concepts and it would be really nice if we could collaborate to help each other.

I hope that you find my posts entertaining and informative and that you gain something out of it. Please feel free to email me about anything that you find is not clear or that is wrong, factually and/or mathematically (I am not a wizard you know!) and I will be prompt to rectify them. So welcome to my blog, welcome to the world of data science and machine leaning!
